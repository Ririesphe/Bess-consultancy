About Us

At BESS Consultancy, we believe education should evolve with the world yet remain grounded in its core purpose: to empower. With over 23 years of combined experience in education, training, and curriculum design, we specialise in:

  • EdTech evaluations that guide smart adoption
  • Curriculum and content development for digital and blended learning
  • Assessment and moderation aligned with national and international standards
  • Facilitation, digital mentorship, and consulting across the education and corporate training sectors

Whether you're a publisher, college, NGO, or training provider, BESS helps you shape learning that is relevant, impactful, and built to last. Our pedagogy is guided by four key values: Integrity. Consideration. Humility. Efficacy. We've supported projects across South Africa and beyond, partnering with local institutions and global education leaders.
